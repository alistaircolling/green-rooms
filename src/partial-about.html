GREEN ROOMS IS A PIONEERING AND UNIQUE ARTS HOTEL, SITUATED IN WOOD GREEN, LONDON, OPENING TO THE PUBLIC IN MAY 2016.

IT WILL BE THE FIRST ARTS-LED INDEPENDENT SOCIAL ENTERPRISE HOTEL IN THE UK.
IT WILL BE A CREATIVE CENTRE AND AN INTEGRAL PART OF THE ARTS COMMUNITY.

WORKING WITH SOME OF LONDON’S LEADING CULTURAL INSTITUTIONS, SUCH AS SOMERSET HOUSE, SOHO THEATRE AND THE ROYAL COURT, GREEN ROOMS WILL ENCOURAGE ITS DIVERSE AND CURIOUS GUESTS TO ENGAGE IN AN EXCITING PROGRAMME OF EXHIBITIONS, PERFORMANCES AND EVENTS IN THE HOTEL’S COMMUNAL AREAS.
GREEN ROOMS HAS BEEN DESIGNED PRINCIPALLY FOR ARTISTS VISITING AND WORKING IN LONDON, AND WILL OFFER AFFORDABLE ACCOMMODATION IN A BEAUTIFUL SETTING THAT INSPIRES CREATIVITY.

PRICES START FROM JUST £18 PER NIGHT.
