STANDARD ROOMS Twelve standard rooms set across the first and second floors. Each features a three quarter bed (perfect for one, or two people who are happy to be close) original wood flooring and simple but stylish furniture. The rooms are served by shared bathroom facilities on both floors.
CORNER ROOMS Four king size doubles and two twin rooms set across the first and second floors. Considerably larger than the standard rooms, each features a king size double bed or two single beds, original wood flooring, vintage furniture and lighting from bespoke interiors label Folk. The rooms are served by shared bathroom facilities on both floors.
EN-SUITE DOUBLES
Situated on the first and second floors, these four en-suite doubles are set on either side of the central staircase. Similar in size and appearance to the corner doubles but with the addition of a private en-suite bathroom.
STUDIO APARTMENTS
There are two studio apartments, both set on the lower ground floor. Ideal for longer stays, the studio apartments have double beds, and workspace areas plus their own en-suite bathrooms. Studio 2 has a kitchenette and Studio 1 is a fully compliant accessible space.
DORMITORY ROOMS
The dormitories at GREEN ROOMS are located on the ground floor behind the main lobby. The two dorms (one with 12 single beds, one with 16) are ideal for large block bookings or individuals happy to engage with and meet other artists. Shared male and female bathroom facilities serve the dormitories and there is an allocated storage locker for each guest. In addition, the dormitories have an adjoining private lounge for guests to congregate and relax with direct access to the outdoor areas.
Green Rooms is a new hotel concept – affordable and stylish, focusing on the things you need rather than the things you don’t. Our rooms are simply and tastefully decorated. There are no TV’s, phones or mini-bars in them, but there are great beds, soft linen and carefully chosen furniture.

We offer free WiFi in all the rooms and public areas. There are no hidden extras in our rates, we always include VAT @ 20% and service. For group bookings of 4 rooms/beds or more, please contact info@greenrooms.london or give us a call on 0208 888 5317
