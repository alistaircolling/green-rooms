DOUBLES
Twelve standard doubles set across the first and second floors. Each features a double bed, original wood flooring and stylish furniture. The rooms are served by shared bathroom facilities.
CORNER ROOMS
Four deluxe doubles and two deluxe twins set across the first and second floors. Considerably larger than the standard doubles, each features a king size double bed, original wood flooring, furniture from bespoke interiors label Folk, ample storage space plus additional work/relax space.
DELUXE DOUBLES
Situated on the first and second floors, these four en- suite doubles are set on either side of the central staircase. Similar in size and appearance to the deluxe doubles but with the addition of a private en- suite washroom.
STUDIO APARTMENTS
There are two studio apartments, both set on the ground floor and connected to the main staircase. Ideal for extended stays, the studio apartments have double beds, adjoining kitchen and workspace areas plus their own en-suite bathrooms. One of these apartments is a fully compliant accessible space.
DORMITORY ROOMS
The dormitories at GREEN ROOMS are located on the ground floor behind the main lobby. The two dorms (one with 12 beds, one with 16) are ideal for large block bookings or individuals happy to engage with and meet other artists. Shared male and female bathroom facilities serve the dormitories and there is an allocated storage locker for each guest. In addition, the dormitories have an adjoining private lounge for guests to congregate and relax with direct access to the outdoor areas.
