Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions page. In the run-up to our launch in Spring 2016 there are all sorts of questions flying around in the virtual air! Hopefully this page answers yours…

HOW CAN WE BOOK A ROOM?


Green Rooms Hotels will open to the public for reservations on April 1st 2016.
Corporate bookings (e.g. theatre companies) are already being taken for groups of more than 4 looking to stay a minimum of 3 nights. For group bookings, send us an email and ask for Sylvia Dietz.


CAN ONLY ARTISTS STAY IN THE HOTEL?


Green Rooms Hotels is for practising artists and people who work in the arts. This does not just entail fine artists (e.g. painters and sculptors), but thespoans, musicians, writers, designers and all manner of creative individuals!


HOW DO YOU KNOW I AM AN ARTIST?


When applying to stay at Green Rooms Hotel you will be asked to give a little bit of information about yourself, your current projects and links if possible to any websites. This will be looked at by our bookings team when processing your reservation request.


CAN I WORK AT GREEN ROOMS HOTEL?


We will be recruiting staff very shortly, keep an eye on our website. Interviews will take place in March.


HOW DO I COLLABORATE WITH GREEN ROOMS?


The best thing to do is to join our mailing list, coming soon, and to send us an email with your contact details and a brief summary of your idea. From launch there will be a busy and vibrant programme of events, exhibitions and shows taking place in and around the hotel – But we will be on the hunt for ideas too, so get in touch!


TELL ME MORE ABOUT THE RESTAURANT


Green Rooms Hotel will showcase a restaurateur programme, nurturing new culinary talents in North London. The first of these will be Columbian Street Kitchen who are already practising nearby in Wood Green


IS THE HOTEL FULLY ACCESSIBLE?


All of the communal areas in the hotel are accessible via elevator, ramps and a chair lift moving between the ground and lower ground. There will be two guest rooms which are fully accessible.


WHO OWNS THE HOTEL?


Green Rooms Hotel is owned and created by Nick Hartwright in partnership with Kurt Bredenbeck, supported by Haringey Council and Mayor of London.
