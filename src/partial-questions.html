Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions page. Hopefully this page answers yours…

HOW CAN WE BOOK A ROOM?


Reservations can be made online via this website. Corporate bookings (e.g. theatre companies) are already being taken for groups of more than 4 looking to stay a minimum of 3 nights. For group bookings, send us an email to info@greenrooms.london

CAN ONLY ARTISTS STAY IN THE HOTEL?


Everyone is welcome at Green Rooms, we are a hotel for Haringey, and for London. However Green Rooms does offer preferential rates for practising artists and people coming to London working in the creative industries. This does not just entail fine artists (e.g. painters and sculptors), but thespians, musicians, writers, designers and all manner of creative individuals!

HOW DO YOU KNOW I AM AN ARTIST?


When applying to stay at Green Rooms you will be asked to give a little bit of information about yourself, your current projects and links if possible to any websites. This will be looked at by our bookings team when processing your reservation request.

CAN I WORK AT GREEN ROOMS?


We will always be on the look-out for talented people who would love to work for us. Keep an eye on twitter and our website for the latest vacancies.

HOW DO I COLLABORATE WITH GREEN ROOMS?


The best thing to do is to join our mailing list, and to send us an email with your contact details and a brief summary of your idea. We are working on a busy and vibrant programme of events, exhibitions and shows taking place in and around the hotel – but we will be on the hunt for ideas too, so get in touch!


TELL ME MORE ABOUT THE RESTAURANT


Green Rooms will showcase a restaurateur programme, nurturing new culinary talents in North London. The first of these will be Colombian Street Kitchen who are already operating nearby in Wood Green.


IS THE HOTEL FULLY ACCESSIBLE?


All of the communal areas in the hotel are accessible via elevator, and ramps moving between the ground and lower ground. There is a studio room which is fully accessible.
